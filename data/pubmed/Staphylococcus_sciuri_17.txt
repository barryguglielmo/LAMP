Staphylococcus sciuri peritonitis in a patient on peritoneal dialysis.

Meservey A(1), Sullivan A(1), Wu C(1), Lantos PM(1).

Author information: 
(1)Duke University School of Medicine, Durham, North Carolina.

Staphylococcus sciuri is an occasional cause of human infection that has been
described in the flora of numerous animal species and in environmental specimens.
Here, we report a rare case of S. sciuri peritonitis in a dialysis patient who
had exposure to peridomestic animals.
