Draft Genome Sequence of Bifidobacterium pseudocatenulatum Bif4, Isolated from
Healthy Infant Feces.

We report the 2.24-Mb draft genome sequence of Bifidobacterium pseudocatenulatum 
Bif4, isolated from a fecal sample from a healthy infant. The specific
annotations revealed genes predictive of its probiotic attributes.
