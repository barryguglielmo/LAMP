Complete Genome Sequence of Flavonifractor plautii JCM 32125T.

We report the complete genome sequence of Flavonifractor plautii JCM 32125T (=VPI
0310T). The genome consists of a single circular chromosome of 3,985,392 bp (G+C 
content, 60.9%) and was predicted to contain 3 complete sets of rRNA genes, 63
tRNA genes, and 3,764 protein-coding sequences.
