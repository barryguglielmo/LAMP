Treatment of Neisseria meningitidis prosthetic joint infections with short-course
antibiotics: three cases and a literature review.

Neisseria meningitidis is a rare cause of prosthetic joint infection (PJI), with 
only three cases previously reported. Here we report three further cases, all of 
which were successfully treated with implant retention and short-course
antibiotics ( < 6  weeks).
