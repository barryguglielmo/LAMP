Draft Genome Sequences of 13 Isolates of Adlercreutzia equolifaciens, Eggerthella
lenta, and Gordonibacter urolithinfaciens, Isolated from Human Fecal Samples in
Karlsruhe, Germany.

Danylec N(#)(1), Stoll DA(#)(2), Göbl A(1), Huch M(1).

Author information:

(#)Contributed equally

Here, we report the annotated draft genome sequences of 13 Eggerthellaceae
strains isolated from fecal samples from two healthy human volunteers in
Karlsruhe, Germany, i.e., Adlercreutzia equolifaciens ResAG-91, Eggerthella lenta
MRI-F 36, MRI-F 37, MRI-F 40, ResAG-49, ResAG-88, ResAG-121, and ResAG-145, and
Gordonibacter urolithinfaciens ResAG-5, ResAG-26, ResAG-43, ResAG-50, and
ResAG-59.
