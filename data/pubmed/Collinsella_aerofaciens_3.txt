We report a complete genome sequence of Collinsella aerofaciens JCM 10188T (=VPI 
1003T). The genome consists of a circular chromosome (2,428,218 bp with 60.6% G+C
content) and two extrachromosomal elements. The genome was predicted to contain 5
sets of rRNA genes, 58 tRNA genes, and 2,079 protein-encoding sequences.
