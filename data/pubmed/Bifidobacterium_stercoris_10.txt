Complete Genome Sequence of Bifidobacterium adolescentis ZJ2, Isolated from a
Centenarian in Anhui, China.

Jin Z(1), Li W(1), Wang W(1), Sun B(2).

Author information: 
(1)Chorain Health Technology Co., Ltd., Hefei, Anhui, China.
(2)School of Life Science, University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, 
Anhui, China sunb@ustc.edu.cn.

In this announcement, we present the complete genome sequence of Bifidobacterium 
adolescentis strain ZJ2, which was isolated from a female centenarian in Anhui,
China. The final genome consists of a 2,401,766-bp chromosome with a G+C content 
of 59.90%.
