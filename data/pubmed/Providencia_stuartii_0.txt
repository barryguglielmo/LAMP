Draft Genome Sequence of a New Delhi Metallo-β-Lactamase (NDM-1)-Producing
Providencia stuartii Strain Isolated in Lima, Peru.

Providencia stuartii is an opportunistic pathogen of the Enterobacteriales order.
Here, we report the 4,594,658-bp draft genome sequence of a New Delhi
metallo-β-lactamase (NDM-1)-producing Providencia stuartii strain that was
isolated from an emergency patient in a private clinic in Lima, Peru.
