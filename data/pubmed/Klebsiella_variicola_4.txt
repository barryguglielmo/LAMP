Draft Genome Sequences of Plant-Pathogenic Klebsiella variicola Strains Isolated 
from Plantain in Haiti.

The genus Klebsiella includes pathogenic and nonpathogenic species. We report the
5.57-Mb genome sequences of two Klebsiella variicola strains, G18-1365 and
G18-1376, isolated from symptomatic plantain plants in Haiti. These strains are
genetically closely related (average nucleotide identity [ANI] > 99%) to the
previously described type strain of K. variicola, DSM 15968.
