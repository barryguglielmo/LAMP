Dialister massiliensis sp. nov., a new bacterium isolated from the human gut.

Afouda P(1)(2), Dubourg G(1)(2), Tomeï E(3)(2), Raoult D(1)(2)(4), Fournier
PE(3)(2).

Author information:

(3)Aix-Marseille Université, IRD, AP-HM, SSA, VITROME, Marseille, France.
(4)Special Infections Agents Unit, King Fahd Medical Research Center, King
Abdulaziz University, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.

Dialister massiliensis strain Marseille-P5638T (= CSUR P5638) is a new species
from the genus Dialister and family Veillonellaceae which was isolated from the
gut microbiota of a healthy individual.
