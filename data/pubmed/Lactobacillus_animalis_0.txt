Complete Genome Sequences of Lactobacillus Strains C25 and P38, Isolated from
Chicken Cecum.

Hassan HM(1), Mendoza M(2), Rezvani M(2), Koci MD(2), Dickey AN(3), Scholl EH(3).

Author information: 
(1)Prestage Department of Poultry Science, North Carolina State University,
Raleigh, North Carolina, USA hmhassan@ncsu.edu.
(2)Prestage Department of Poultry Science, North Carolina State University,
Raleigh, North Carolina, USA.
(3)Bioinformatics Research Center, North Carolina State University, Raleigh,
North Carolina, USA.

We report the complete circular genome sequences of Lactobacillus crispatus
strain C25, its plasmid, and Lactobacillus animalis strain P38; both strains were
isolated from the cecum of 4-week-old chickens. These isolates represent
potential probiotic strains for poultry.
