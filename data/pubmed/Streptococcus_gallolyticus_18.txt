Multifocal extracranial mycotic aneurysms in a patient with endocarditis.

Spring AM(1), Catalano MA(1), Farber BF(2), Krishnasastry KV(3), Yu PJ(1).

Author information: 
(1)Division of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery, Northwell Health, Manhasset, 
NY, United States.
(2)Division of Infectious Disease, Department of Medicine, Northwell Health,
Manhasset, NY, United States.
(3)Division of Vascular Surgery, Department of Surgery, Northwell Health,
Manhasset, NY, United States.

This report describes the case of a 44-year-old man with Streptococcus
gallolyticus (formerly S. bovis) endocarditis requiring surgical mitral valve
replacement who developed multifocal extracranial mycotic aneurysms after the
resolution of infection.
