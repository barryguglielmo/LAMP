Nonpuerperal breast abscess due to Prevotella bivia.

Contrary to puerperal abscess, nonpuerperal breast abscess is often caused by
anaerobic bacteria; polymicrobial aerobic-anaerobic infections are also frequent.
Empiric first-choice treatment with broad-spectrum antibiotics should be
considered.
