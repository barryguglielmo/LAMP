Complete Genome Sequences of Two Salmonella enterica Lytic Phages, NBSal006 and
NBSal007.

Llanos CD(1), Punil R(2), Mamani E(2), Mormontoy C(2), Bardales JA(3).

Author information: 
(1)Nextbiotics, Inc., Oakland, California, USA.
(2)Departamento de Microbiología y Parasitología, Universidad Nacional Mayor de
San Marcos, Lima, Peru.
(3)Nextbiotics, Inc., Oakland, California, USA jorge@next-biotics.com.

The use of bacteriophages as antimicrobial agents represents a promising
alternative for the control of pathogenic bacteria. Here, we present the complete
genome sequences of two novel Salmonella enterica lytic bacteriophages, NBSal006 
and NBSal007, candidates for Salmonella biocontrol.
