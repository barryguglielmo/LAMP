Draft Genome Sequence of Pseudarthrobacter sp. Strain ATCC 49442 (Formerly
Micrococcus luteus), a Pyridine-Degrading Bacterium.

Gupta N(1)(2), Skinner KA(1), Summers ZM(3), Edirisinghe JN(1)(2), Weisenhorn
PB(1), Faria JP(1)(2), Marshall CW(1), Sharma A(2), Gottel NR(2), Gilbert
JA(1)(2), Henry CS(1)(2), O'Loughlin EJ(4).

Author information: 
(1)Argonne National Laboratory, Lemont, Illinois, USA.
(2)University of Chicago, Chicago, Illinois, USA.
(3)ExxonMobil Research and Engineering Company, Annandale, New Jersey, USA.
(4)Argonne National Laboratory, Lemont, Illinois, USA oloughlin@anl.gov.

We present here the draft genome sequence of a pyridine-degrading bacterium,
Micrococcus luteus ATCC 49442, which was reclassified as Pseudarthrobacter sp.
strain ATCC 49442 based on its draft genome sequence. Its genome length is
4.98 Mbp, with 64.81% GC content.
