Complete Genome Sequence of Thermophilic Bacterium Aeribacillus pallidus PI8.

Kita K(1), Ishida A(1), Tanaka K(1), Ishikawa S(1), Yoshida KI(2).

Author information: 
(1)Department of Science, Technology and Innovation, Kobe University, Kobe,
Hyogo, Japan.
(2)Department of Science, Technology and Innovation, Kobe University, Kobe,
Hyogo, Japan kenyoshi@kobe-u.ac.jp.

Here, we report the complete genome sequence of Aeribacillus pallidus PI8, a
thermophilic bacterium, isolated from soybean stem extract. The sequence was
determined using Illumina and Nanopore sequencers. Bioinformatic analyses of the 
genome sequence revealed the presence of possible bacteriocin gene clusters.
