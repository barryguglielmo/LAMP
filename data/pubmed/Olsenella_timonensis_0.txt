Olsenella timonensis sp. nov., a new bacteria species isolated from the human gut
microbiota.

Ndongo S(1), Tall ML(1), Ngom II(1), Delerce J(1), Levasseur A(1), Raoult
D(1)(2), Fournier PE(3), Khelaifia S(1).

Author information: 
(1)Aix-Marseille Univ, IRD, APHM, MEPHI, Marseille, France.
(2)Institut Hospitalo-Universitaire Méditerranée Infection, Marseillle, France.
(3)UMR VITROME, Aix Marseille Université, IRD, SSA, AP-HM, Marseille, France.

Olsenella timonensis sp. nov., strain Marseille-P2300T (= CSUR
P2300; =DSM102072), is a new bacterial species from the phylum Firmicutes in the 
family Atopobiaceae.This bacteria species was isolated from the human gut
microbiota.
