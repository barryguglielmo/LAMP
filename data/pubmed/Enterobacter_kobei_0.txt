Draft Genome Sequence of Enterobacter kobei M4-VN, Isolated from Potatoes with
Soft Rot Disease.

Enterobacter kobei M4-VN, isolated from potatoes with soft rot disease in
Vietnam, contains a total of 4,754,309 bp with 4,424 predicted coding sequences
and a G+C content of 55.1%.
