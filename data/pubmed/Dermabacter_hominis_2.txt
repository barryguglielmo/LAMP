Molecular identification of Dermatobia hominis (Diptera: Oestridae): a neglected 
agent causing myiasis in Mexico.

Myiasis represents a group of neglected tropical diseases caused by the
infestation of vertebrate tissues by dipterous larvae. We herein report an
imported case of foruncular myasis caused by Dermatobia hominis in Mexico City.
The species was confirmed by DNA sequencing and phylogenetic reconstruction
analysis.
