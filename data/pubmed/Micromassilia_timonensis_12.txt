Massilistercora timonensis gen. nov., sp. nov., a new bacterium isolated from the
human microbiota.

Massilistercora timonensis gen. nov., sp. nov. strain Marseille-P3756T is a new
species of the phylum Firmicutes; it was isolated from the human gut microbiota
and has a genome of 2 769 591 bp (51.2% G + C). The closest species based on 16S 
rRNA sequence was Merdimonas faecis strain BR31 with 95.2 % sequence similarity. 
Considering phenotypic features and comparative genome studies, we proposed the
strain Marseille-P3756T as the type strain of Massilistercora timonensis sp.
nov., a new species within the genus Massilistercora.
