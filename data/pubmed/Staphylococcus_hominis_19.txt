Members of the Staphylococcus genus are known pathogens causing mastitis in dairy
cows, which results in major economic losses. Here, we present Staphylococcus
epidermidis UFMG-H7, Staphylococcus hominis UFMG-H7B, and Staphylococcus sciuri
UFMG-H6, isolated from the urine of healthy purebred Gyr heifers.
