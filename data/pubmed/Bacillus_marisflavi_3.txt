Here, we report the 4.34-Mb draft genome assembly of Bacillus marisflavi
CK-NBRI-03 (or P3), a Gram-positive bacterium, with an average G+C content of
48.66%. P3 was isolated from agricultural soil from the Badaun (midwestern plain 
zone) region of Uttar Pradesh, India.
