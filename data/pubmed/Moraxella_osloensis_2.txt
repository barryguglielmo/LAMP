Complete Genome Sequence of Moraxella osloensis Strain YV1, Isolated from an
Australian Wastewater Treatment Plant.

Batinovic S(1), Rice DTF(1), Seviour RJ(1), Petrovski S(2).

Author information: 
(1)Department of Physiology, Anatomy, and Microbiology, La Trobe University,
Bundoora, Australia.
(2)Department of Physiology, Anatomy, and Microbiology, La Trobe University,
Bundoora, Australia steve.petrovski@latrobe.edu.au.

We report the complete genome sequence of Moraxella osloensis strain YV1, which
was isolated from a wastewater treatment plant in Australia. The YV1 genome
comprises a 2,615,801-bp chromosome and four plasmids. Moraxella osloensis strain
YV1 displays the distinctive morphology of Eikelboom morphotype 1863.
