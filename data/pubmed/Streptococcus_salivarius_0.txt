Complete Genome Sequence of Streptococcus salivarius DB-B5, a Novel Probiotic
Candidate Isolated from the Supragingival Plaque of a Healthy Female Subject.

Fields FR(1)(2), Li X(1), Navarre WW(2), Naito M(3).

Author information: 
(1)Dose Biosystems, Inc., Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
(2)Department of Molecular Genetics, University of Toronto, Toronto, Ontario,
Canada.
(3)Dose Biosystems, Inc., Toronto, Ontario, Canada mizue@dosebiosystems.com.

Streptococcus salivarius DB-B5 was isolated from the supragingival plaque of a
healthy female subject. The complete 2.3-Mb genome consists of one circular
chromosome, two circular plasmids (including a megaplasmid), and one linear
phage-like episome. The genome possesses two separate loci encoding bacteriocins.
