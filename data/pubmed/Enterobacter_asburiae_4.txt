Draft Genome Sequence of Enterobacter asburiae UFMG-H9, Isolated from Urine from 
a Healthy Bovine Heifer (Gyr Breed).

Enterobacter asburiae is part of the Enterobacter cloacae complex, related to
nosocomial opportunistic infections in humans. Here, we report the draft genome
of E. asburiae strain UFMG-H9, an isolate from urine from a healthy Gyr heifer.
