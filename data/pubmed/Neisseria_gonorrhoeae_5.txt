A culture collection of 50 Neisseria gonorrhoeae isolates is available from the
CDC & FDA Antibiotic Resistance Isolate Bank. Associated data include antibiotic 
susceptibility information for azithromycin, cefixime, cefpodoxime, ceftriaxone, 
tetracycline, ciprofloxacin, penicillin, and spectinomycin and linked
whole-genome sequences.
