Anaerosphaera massiliensis sp. nov., a new bacterium isolated from the stool of a
39-year-old Pygmy.

Khalil JY(2).

Anaerosphaera massiliensis strain Marseille-P4592T (= CSURP4592T; = CCUG72452T)
is a new species isolated from the stool of a 39-year-old male Pygmy from the
Democratic Republic of Congo.
