Draft Genome Sequence of Paenibacillus polymyxa 3A-25AI, a Strain Antagonist to
Root Rot Causal Phytopathogens.

Here, we report the draft genome sequence of the Paenibacillus polymyxa 3A-25AI
strain, isolated from the rhizosphere of wild grass. This strain inhibits
Phytophthora capsici and Rhizoctonia solani phytopathogens. The genome size is
5.6 Mb, with a G+C content of 45.59%, and contains 5,079 genes, 4,968 coding DNA 
sequences (CDSs), 35 tRNAs, 3 rRNAs, and 72 unexpected miscellaneous RNA
(miscRNA) features.
