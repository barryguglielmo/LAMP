Complete Genome Sequence of Lactobacillus harbinensis Strain NSMJ42, Isolated
from Makgeolli, a Traditional Korean Alcoholic Beverage.

Jung JY(1), Han SS(2), Kim ZH(2), Ryu BG(2), Jin HM(2), Chung EJ(2).

Author information: 
(1)Microbial Research Department, Nakdonggang National Institute of Biological
Resources (NNIBR), Sangju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea jyjung@nnibr.re.kr.
(2)Microbial Research Department, Nakdonggang National Institute of Biological
Resources (NNIBR), Sangju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea.

In the present work, we report the complete genome sequence of Lactobacillus
harbinensis NSMJ42, isolated from makgeolli (a Korean traditional alcoholic
beverage) in South Korea. The final genome assembly consists of a 3.29-Mbp
chromosome with 3,082 protein-coding sequences and a G+C content of 53.36%.
