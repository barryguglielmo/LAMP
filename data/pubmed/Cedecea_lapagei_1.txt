First report of pneumonia and septic shock caused by Cedecea lapagei in Vietnam.

Hai PD(1), Dung NM(1), Tot NH(1), Chinh NX(1), Thuyet BT(2), Hoa LTV(3), Son
PN(4), Thanh LV(5).

Author information: 
(1)Intensive Care Unit, 108 Military Central Hospital, Viet Nam.
(2)Department of Microbiology, 108 Military Central Hospital, Viet Nam.
(3)Intensive Care Unit, Tam Anh Hospital, Viet Nam.
(4)Department of Cardiology, 108 Military Central Hospital, Viet Nam.
(5)108 Military Central Hospital, Ha Noi, Vietnam.

Cedecea lapagei is rarely known to cause infections in humans. We report the
first case of pneumonia and septic shock caused by Cedecea lapagei in a
38-year-old man in Vietnam. Cedecea lapagei may be an emerging infectious agent
in humans.
