Draft Genome Sequence of Escherichia marmotae E690, Isolated from Beef Cattle.

We report here the draft genome sequence of an extended-spectrum β-lactamase
(ESBL)-producing Escherichia species isolated from rectal feces collected from
beef cattle in northern Spain. Analysis of the draft genome identified the strain
as a member of the newly described species Escherichia marmotae.
